Business Plan

The Good Samaritans believe that startups can have a great impact on both the business as well as the social sector. We believe our website The Good Samaritans can make a positive difference in the lives of children suffering from learning difficulties.

Children with learning difficulties such as dyslexia often have a problem retaining words. We focus on teaching children phonetics through a web-based platform with the help of personalized books and other learning materials.

I was the Co-Founder and Chief Marketing Officer in charge of handling the start-ups social media handles as well as maintaining regular blogs and additional information on our website.

We participated in an online competition- Kontingence; where we had to make a business plan and present it during an interview. Our team won 3rd position among 35+ countries and 500+ participants.
