I designed a personal portfolio for my final GBDA 101 assignment showcasing all my projects as well as reflection on what I have learnt in the course.

The theme of my portfolio was Meraki which means ‘to do something with soul, creativity and love; to put something of yourself into your work’.

I used various different design techniques and principles such as grids, typography, storytelling, interaction design, emotional design hierarchy, GESTALT principles etc.
