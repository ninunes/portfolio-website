Portfolio Website

I designed and built an interactice portfolio website using HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript. I hosted this webpage on GitHub.

Initially I faced a lot of issues that required me to spend days debugging the problems but as I progressed I realised how much I learnt since high-school and how I can apply those lessons in different ways.

I am not from a CS background but once I set my mind to this I knew I couldn't give up until it was complete. Creating this website was a personal achievement since it took me days of trial and error, tutorials effects only to find out that creating a website isnt as hard as I thought it would be!

This website has different features such as; a downloadable resume, gives people a little more idea about who I am, showcases my skills and projects as well as how to contact me.
